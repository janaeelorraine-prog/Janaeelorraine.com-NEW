AstroOdu Certification

Five levels. Each gates the next. Becoming a credentialed reader within the lineage is not a purchase — it is a calling that must be confirmed.

Certification is not a transaction. It is a recognition — that you have walked the framework, demonstrated the skill, and been confirmed by the lineage to carry the work to others. Application is required at every level. Approval is required before payment. No exceptions.
